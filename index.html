About Me

I obtained Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering from the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2020. My Ph.D. supervisor was Prof. Irwin King. Before that, I received my B.E. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2015 under the supervision of Prof. Kenny Q. Zhu and Prof. Shi-Lin Wang. Now, I'm an applied scientist of AWS.
