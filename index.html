About Me

I'm currently a software engineer in AI and System Research Group at Google. Before that, I was an applied scientist at AWS, where I led model development for analytics, tabular data, and graph ML. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering from the Chinese University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Irwin King and received my B.E. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
